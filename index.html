Trust & Psychological Safety - "No surprises" commitment to transparent information sharing
- Safe challenge and constructive conflict as positive norms
- Peer support in high-stakes decisions and difficult conversations 		Monitor team energy and safety monthly. Use pulse surveys and regular check-ins on team health.
Accountability - Shared team metrics with public review cadence
- Timely follow-up on commitments and misses
- Blameless system correction as default response 		Distinguish between accountability to outcomes (goals) and to each other (team behaviors). Treat misses as learning triggers, not blame.
Collaboration & Feedback - Scheduled cross-functional workshops and regular reflection sessions
- Real-time, actionable feedback both peer-to-peer and as a group
- Open invitation to coach or be coached at any level 		Systematize reflection: short learning reviews after milestones and at least one deep retrospective per quarter.
Decision-Making - Transparent criteria for decision rights and escalation
- Documented, reviewed decision log
- Fast, iterative experimentation encouraged when uncertainty is high 		Create agreed guardrails for which decisions need full consensus vs. empowered delegation within roles.
